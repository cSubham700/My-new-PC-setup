My New PC Setup

Recently I have build my new PC setup. Around 20 days ago I decided to build it by purchasing components from online. Because of high price rate and deayed service I decided to assemble it from offline market. As I don't need a lot rather than coding and some minor editing work I decided to build a budget PC.
